About SAAS


Our Mission at Seattle Academy is to prepare students for college and life. Seattle Academy of Arts and Sciences is an independent secondary school with a mission to prepare our students to participate effectively in modern society. We, therefore, seek a diversified student body and faculty. We offer a demanding college-preparatory curriculum integrating the arts and emphasizing a global perspective. We utilize the resources of our urban environment to extend our classrooms, to enhance our programs, and to engage our students in public service. Most of all, we seek to graduate motivated young men and women of talent and integrity who are prepared to contribute productively to a changing world.


Seattle Academy provides a broad education in academics, arts, athletics, and community involvement. Our students take their curricular work very seriously—getting good grades, the arts, sports, speech and debate, mock trial, drama, and dance are all “cool.”


Our Values


VISION:Central to a Seattle Academy education is the Culture of Performance. Students are asked to demonstrate over and over – often reaching for professional standards – what they are learning. Students discover the thrill of achievement and the lessons of failure, and they come to understand that the greater the risk, the greater the reward.


GOAL:Meeting the challenge to integrate and apply skills and knowledge – whether in the classroom, on the athletic field, or on the stage – expands personal horizons and fosters greater self-esteem, confidence, and maturity. For example, a junior biology student may conceive, develop, and orally defend a scientific hypothesis to a jury of parents or experts in the field. In doing so, that student puts to use knowledge learned in a full range of his or her classes: Knowledge from textbooks and the laboratory, creative thinking encouraged in the art studio, presentation skills enlivened on the stage, and the courage to take risks developed on the athletic field or on a wilderness trip. This integration and performance happens over and over, in all disciplines and all grades.
